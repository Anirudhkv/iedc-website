Events

Kickstart

This introductory event is conducted annually and it intends to bring forward the Startup interests among the students in our college. Interactive sessions with various entrepreneurs and other fun activities make kickstart a memorable event for all the participants.

Product Expo

In connection with Insignia, CSE department's technical fest conducted as a part of Dyuthi, the national techno-cultural multifest of GEC Thrissur, IEDC GECT organized an intercollegiate product expo, inviting project proposals from student and startup communities. Open source, hardware and software based ideas were presented by various teams and the winners won prizes worth 40K and internships from ICFOSS.

Workshop Series

Every year, IEDC conducts a series of hands-on workshops for all the departments in the college. Various technologies are introduced to the students and they are given various platforms to identify their interested field.

GEC Hacks

This is the annual hackathon event organised for the students to develop their innovative ideas and present the same. The event grants them the opportunity to implement their dream project, as we support the winning teams with adequate facilities to develop their idea. The first edition of GEC hacks was conducted online.

Ad Ingenium

This is an online lecture series that features the eminent alumni of GEC who have left their signature in various technological and innovative fields. The students, staff and the faculty can interact with them as they guide the listeners to newer perspectives.

Tales of Triumphs

This is an online series that features the stories of those innovators and entrepreneurs who have contributed impeccably to the society and the technological world. The first edition of this series featured the alumni of GEC
